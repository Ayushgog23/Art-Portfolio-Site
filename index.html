About Ayush

Ayush is a Canadian artist who uses a variety of mediums in his work, Colored pencil, acrylic, and digital media being his specialty. Each of his pieces may aim to share events and characteristics of his life, tell stories, and provoke a unique feeling inside the viewer by contrasting and differentiating from color, shape, and form found in the real world. Alongside his art, Ayush follows his path in the field of computer science, hoping to incorporate aspects of this field into his art in the future, and vice versa!

Scotts Sick and Show Campaign

As a custom artist, Ayush has been working with Scotts Miracle Grow on a yearly basis to create a shoe that would not only help the company reach marketing goals, but also increase customer interaction. The custom grass stained shoes are made using leather paint and go to the raffle winner of the Sick and Show campaign.
